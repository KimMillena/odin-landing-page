Tulips: More Than Just Beauty

Each tulip color tells a story — of love, friendship, or new beginnings, find out what each tulip color symbolize.

Which Tulip Are You?

Pink tulips symbolizes affection, caring, good wishes, and love.

White tulips represents purify, forgiveness, respect, honor, and new beginnings.

Yellow tulips symbolizes happiness, cheerfulness, and hope.

Purple tulips symbolizes royalty, elegance, and admiration.
